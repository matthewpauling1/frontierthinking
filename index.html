About Frontier Thinking

Frontier Thinking is an innovative educational consultancy focused on teaching higher-order thinking skills to students and professionals across Utah. Founded on the principle that how people think is as important as what they know, Frontier Thinking addresses the growing demand for critical thinking, creative problem-solving, and cognitive flexibility in an AI-driven economy.

The business concept responds to a crucial gap in traditional education, which often emphasizes knowledge acquisition over thinking methodologies. As artificial intelligence continues to automate knowledge-based tasks, the ability to think critically, identify biases, and solve complex problems becomes increasingly valuable in both academic and professional environments.

Our Vision

Frontier Thinking envisions a Utah where higher-order thinking skills are recognized as essential education, where every student graduates with the cognitive tools to navigate complexity, where professionals approach challenges with reasoned creativity, and where organizations make decisions free from cognitive biases—creating a community of exceptional thinkers prepared to lead in the wisdom economy.
